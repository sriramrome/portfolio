My Beliefs

Explore My
Founder Philosophy

Over the time I've been building, I've had the opportunity to work with great people, fail fast, and learn faster. Here's what I believe.

"The gap between idea and execution is where most people live. I try to close it every single day."
— Sriram Rangaraaj, Founder · Tradesala
01
Execution is the only real moat.
Ideas are commodities. The gap between a founder and a dreamer is the willingness to ship — imperfectly, early, and often. Perfect is the enemy of shipped.
02
India's startup moment is happening right now.
We're at an inflection point — digital infrastructure, a growing middle class, a generation that thinks local-first. The opportunity is real. But only for founders who show up.
03
Community is the unfair advantage.
Products get copied. Communities don't. Build with people, not just for them. The startups that win the next decade will have believers, not just users.
04
Solve the problem in front of you.
Stop chasing trends. Find the thing that's broken — the gap nobody's filling, the problem people have normalized. Fix that. It's enough to build a company on.
05
Transparency builds trust faster than marketing.
Building in public isn't a strategy — it's a commitment to honesty. People support founders they trust, not founders they're impressed by.
